CulturePass Australia is a cultural platform for discovering, creating, and connecting through cultural experiences across Australian states, cities, and councils — with First Nations voices and Welcome to Country at the centre.

## What CulturePass is

CulturePass is a cross-platform app (iOS, Android, and web) where people can:

- **Discover** hubs and events near them, filtered by state, council, and interests.
- **Create** organiser hubs, public events, and Professional Public Profiles through guided wizards.
- **Connect** with other members through messaging, RSVPs, notifications, and tickets.
- **Honour** the traditional custodians of the lands events take place on, through prominent Acknowledgement of Country and hub-supplied Welcome to Country.

## Built with

- **Expo (React Native + Web)** on Expo SDK 53 — one codebase across iOS, Android, and web.
- **Supabase** — Postgres, Auth, Storage, and Edge Functions.
- **TanStack Query** for server state, **Zustand** for client state, **Zod** for validation.
- **NativeWind + Tailwind** for the design system, with **Inter** as the type face.
